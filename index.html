About The Workshop

The term "open source" refers to something people can modify and share because its design is publicly accessible.

FOSS software is software with source code that anyone can inspect, modify, enhance and use free of cost. With this in mind, FOSSEE aims to reduce dependency on proprietary software in educational institutions.

They encourage the use of FOSS tools through various activities to ensure commercial software is replaced by equivalent FOSS tools. They also develop new FOSS tools and upgrade existing tools to meet requirements in academia and research. The FOSSEE project is part of the National Mission on Education through Information and Communication Technology (ICT), Ministry of Human Resources and Development, Government of India.

Statistics

FOSSEE believes in the concept of FOSS even when it comes to learning and has conducted a number of workshops.

A variety of workshops have been conducted on leading technologies like Python, its applications in scientic computing, arduino,etc. They have collaborated with organisations like Python, Scilab, etc on the same.

A total of over 1000 workshops have been conducted by FOSSEE in association with popular organisations like Python, OpenFOAM, eSims,etc, which are also their textbook companions.

MOTTO OF FOSSEE

Awareness: To spread awareness on open source and the usage of FOSS tools among the community.

Promotion: To promote the usage of FOSS tools to help improve the quality of education.

Hands-on experience: To provide hands-on experience on the usage of FOSS tools

Scilab

Scilab is a free and open source software for numerical computaion developed by Scilab Enterprises , France. Scilab also includes Xcos which is an open source alternative to Simulink. To install Scilab, open the terminal and type 'sudo apt-get install scilab'

Python

Python is a general-purpose high-level remarkably powerful dynamic programming language that is used in wide variety of applications domain . It supports multiple programming paradigms .

Open Foam

Open Foam ia a free and open source CFD toolbox used to solve anything from complex fluids flows involving chemical reactions , turblence and heat transfer to solid dynamics and electromagnets .

Sci Lab Arduino

The Scilab-arduino interface is devoted to the control of arduino from scilab.The interface help user to perform embded systems experiments on arduino using scilab code and also through GUI based simulation enviornment , Xcos .
